# Office Risk Taxonomy

## R1: Polished But Unsupported

The artifact looks complete, but claims do not map to sources.

## R2: Stale Number

A current slide or model includes an old number, old chart, old plan, or superseded draft.

## R3: Formula Drift

Parallel formulas point to different rows, columns, or years without explanation.

## R4: Static Model

A workbook looks like a model but contains hardcoded outputs instead of live formulas.

## R5: Mixed Actuals And Plan

Actuals, budgets, forecasts, and estimates are blended without labels.

## R6: Untraceable Chart

A chart is presentation-ready, but its source data, transformation, or date range is unclear.

## R7: Assumption As Fact

The artifact presents an estimate, inference, or placeholder as confirmed.

## R8: Review Theater

The artifact says it has been reviewed, but no claim map, checks tab, or issue log exists.
