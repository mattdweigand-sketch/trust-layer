# Prompt 03: Workbook Doctor

Review this workbook or workbook specification as a skeptical spreadsheet reviewer.

Check:

- raw data is separated from calculations
- assumptions are explicit and labeled
- formulas are consistent across parallel rows and columns
- calculated outputs are not hardcoded
- date ranges are clear
- units are consistent
- charts map to traceable source ranges
- checks exist for totals, duplicates, errors, stale dates, and scenario behavior

Produce:

1. Workbook map
2. Formula risk scan
3. Hardcode risk scan
4. Assumption log
5. Missing evidence list
6. Checks tab recommendations
7. Final verification memo

Do not fix the workbook. Enumerate issues.
