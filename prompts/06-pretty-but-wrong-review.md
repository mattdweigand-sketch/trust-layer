# Prompt 06: Pretty-But-Wrong Review

Read this deck, workbook, or document as a skeptical reviewer who suspects every claim and every number.

For each slide, sheet, section, chart, table, and material claim, identify:

- claims without source attribution
- numbers without a date, owner, or source
- charts whose underlying data is not traceable
- formulas inconsistent across parallel rows or columns
- hardcoded outputs where formulas should exist
- assumptions presented as facts
- stale or mixed date ranges
- source conflicts
- claims that require human judgment

Produce a written issue list.

Do not fix anything.
Do not beautify anything.
Do not rewrite the artifact.

Enumerate issues only.
