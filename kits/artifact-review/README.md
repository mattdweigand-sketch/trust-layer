# Artifact Review Kit

Use this kit after artifact creation and before sharing.

## Reviewer Role

The reviewer is skeptical. It assumes every claim, number, formula, and chart may be wrong.

## Output

- issue list
- severity
- artifact location
- source IDs involved
- recommended owner
- human decision required

## Rule

The review pass enumerates issues. It does not rewrite or beautify the artifact.

## Severity

- `low`: clarity or formatting risk
- `medium`: evidence or traceability risk
- `high`: decision-critical claim, number, formula, or chart cannot be trusted
